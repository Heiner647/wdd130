Our story

In a valley at the foot of the Rocky Mountains just east of Yellowstone National Park in northern Wyoming is a small farm where Clarence and Bessie Zeller raised their family of 3 sons and 3 daughters. To support the family, they produced honey. Honey like no other in the whole world! This honey is made by bees they call “Little Johnnies”.
